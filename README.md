# health_mate

## Project Overview (objective & features).
The primary objective of the HealthMate mobile application is to empower individuals to monitor and
manage their daily health activities through a simple, intuitive interface. The app is designed to promote
healthier lifestyles by enabling users to log and visualize key wellness metrics such as steps walked,
calories burned, and water intake. By providing a centralized platform for health tracking, HealthMate
encourages users to stay consistent with their fitness goals and make informed decisions about their
physical well-being.
